Guidelines for Different Disasters

Earthquake

During an earthquake, drop to your hands and knees, cover your head, and hold on to your shelter until shaking stops.

Flood

Move to higher ground and stay away from floodwaters. Do not drive through flooded areas.

Forest Fire

Create a defensible space around your home, clear away flammable materials, and have an evacuation plan in place.

Rainfall

Monitor weather alerts, ensure proper drainage around your home, and avoid driving in heavy rain.

Volcanic Eruption

Stay indoors, keep windows and doors closed, and follow evacuation orders from authorities.

Tornado

Seek shelter in a basement or an interior room on the lowest floor. Avoid windows and stay away from large objects.

Hurricane

Prepare an emergency kit, secure your home, and follow evacuation orders if necessary.

Heatwave

Stay hydrated, avoid outdoor activities during peak heat, and use fans or air conditioning to stay cool.
